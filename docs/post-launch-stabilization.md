# Post-Launch Stabilization Plan

## Stabilization Window

- Duration: 2 to 4 weeks after public launch.
- Daily triage for release-tagged issues.
- Prioritize crash, startup, and data-safety defects.

## Service Objectives

- Hotfix SLA: same-day triage, targeted patch in 24-72 hours for severe issues.
- Dependency/security queue reviewed at least twice per week.

## Exit Criteria

- Crash and ANR rates are stable.
- No unresolved SEV-1 incidents.
- Maintainer operations cadence is sustainable.

## Next Phase Trigger

Re-evaluate cloud/webhook re-enable only after stabilization exit criteria are met.
